AUGUSTINE VON TRAPP

I like to write all kids of things. Mostly code, often music and hopefully play or two. I have worked as a composer for the last 15 years and have decided to delve deeper into my other passion, coding.

Education: It's been an unusual one for sure. A parade of tutors, a blur of everlasting feild trips and endless hours of autodidactic study. The reason for such an unconventional education is because I started working at a young age.

Work History: I was a songwriter and performer for 15 years. I was fortunate to work with many amazing groups like Pink Martini and numerous nationally recongnized symphonies.

Why I am taking a coding class: I wanted to develop the logical and analytical part of my brain. I realized that coding was a wonderful combination of creative possiblity mixed with technicall skill.
